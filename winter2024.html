Hello everyone! I was quite excited to get creations from the community. You never know what you will get. I am a big fan of generative art and I am always looking for new ways to create art. This time around I wanted to create a more participatory experience. I wanted those who recieved the mail to join in the fun.

Paper

The paper used in this project was hand made (I did not make it personally) this time; definitely can appreciate the amount of effort involved to create one's own paper. Not only the smoothness but the absorptive properties of the paper is quite interesting.

Ink

I used ink from Birmingham Fountain Pens out of PA. These are beautiful inks. The aborption of the ink into the paper is quite interesting.
