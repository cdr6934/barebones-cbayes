Generative Art

Generative art to me is just an art form that I can understand and find a connection as it embodies the beauty of nature through it's seemingly random yet ordered patterns. It works for me as a metaphor of the world around and how sometimes unordered yet ordered it can be. Exploration of Natural and Procedural Phenomena
